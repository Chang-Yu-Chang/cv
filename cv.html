Chang-Yu Chang

My research interest lies in the intersection of systems biology and community ecology. I am interested in combining theory and experiment to study how microbes interact with each other and form an ecological community with versatile functions.

I am currently working on building R and Python packages to simulate dynamics of microbial communities exploitating species, or algorithm for finding optimal community selection strategy.
